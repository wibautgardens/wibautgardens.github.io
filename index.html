Amsterdam Oost - 98 m2

On the third floor (with an elevator!) of the iconic Holland Veste building, you’ll find an apartment that’s bright, airy and spacious. Through the hallway to the left, you’ll reach a large open plan living and dining area with a comfy corner sofa, wide-screen TV and dining table that seats six people comfortably. The kitchen is also open-plan, just off the dining area, where your guests can sit at the breakfast bar and enjoy a glass of wine while you cook in the recently renovated kitchen – featuring a brand-new induction stove, proper oven and American-style fridge.

Just off the living room is the guest bedroom, with a small double bed and a large workspace. In the opposite direction down the hallway, you’ll reach the master bedroom with queen-sized bed and another room that houses the second workspace overlooking the balcony. There’s also a large bathroom with a bathtub and separate shower, as well as double sinks. And in a separate room, the toilet and another small sink.

But Wibaut Gardens’ best feature is its outdoor space. At the back of the apartment is a covered balcony that’s great for a sundowner, even in winter. Downstairs, the building gives way to a huge communal garden (shared only by the apartments) with picnic tables, children’s play equipment and BBQs. The apartment has its own separate terrace within the garden, for extra privacy.

The Neighbourhood

Situated between the Amstel River/Weesperzijde on one side and the Wibautstraat on the other, the apartment is ideally located. It’s right next to Wibautstraat metro station, for easy public transport to Central Station and Schiphol Airport. It’s also close to tram 3, which links the east and west of the city. The neighbourhood is vibrant (but not overrun with tourists) and there are supermarkets, shops, restaurants and bars within easy walking distance. To the east is the rest of Amsterdam Oost, while to the west is De Pijp – both lively areas with their own distinct characters.
